Size matters - adapting to the new UK company thresholds

In December 2024, regulations were made in the UK that increased certain monetary thresholds that form part of the criteria for determining the size of an entity for financial years beginning on or after 6 April 2025.

Join our webinar to explore what these recent changes mean for your role and your financial reporting obligations.

This webinar will explore:

  • The key changes that came into force on 6 April 2025 and their impact on companies;
  • The amendment to Section 1 scope and appendix III Note on legal requirements;
  • The amendments to FRS 105 applicable to the Micro-entities Regime.
