How to identify a fraudulent website / social media pages

  • Check the URL: Always ensure you are on our official website by checking the URL in your browserâ€™s address bar. Our official website is https://www.croneri.co.uk. Be cautious of any slight variations in the URL.
  • Look for Secure Connections: Our website uses HTTPS to secure your data. Look for a padlock symbol in the address bar and ensure the URL begins with "https://".
  • Verify Contact Information: Fraudulent websites often lack proper contact information. Ensure that the contact details match those listed on our official site.
  • Examine the Website Design: While fraudulent sites may look similar, they often have subtle differences in design and layout. Compare the site with our official website to spot any inconsistencies.
  • Check that the social media account is verified: This can be easily determined by the blue tick badge that we use on our social media accounts.
  • Look for improper spelling or grammar: Inconsistencies within the website could mean the website has been quickly set up without attention to detail.
  • Be aware of a lot of advertising pop-ups:, Look out for calls for urgent action before a time limit is reached, such as expiring discounts or sales.

What to do if you encounter a fraudulent website / social media pages

  • Do Not Enter Personal Information: If you suspect a website is fraudulent, do not enter any personal or payment information.
  • Report the Website: Please report any suspicious websites to us immediately at client.experience@croneri.co.uk. Your vigilance helps us protect other customers.
  • Report the Social Media Page: Please report any suspicious social media pages as scam or fake pages.
  • Contact Your Bank: If you have entered payment information on a fraudulent site, contact your bank or credit card provider immediately to secure your accounts.
