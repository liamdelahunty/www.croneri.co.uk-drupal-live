British Safety Council is dedicated to making sure no-one is injured or made ill through their work.

For over sixty years it has been the trusted guide to excellent health, safety, wellbeing and environmental management through training, auditing, consultancy and, of course, their membership services.

Since its foundation in 1957, British Safety Council has campaigned tirelessly to protect workers from accidents, hazards and unsafe conditions, and played a decisive role in the political process that has led to adoption of landmark safety legislation in the UK.

Its members in more than 60 countries are committed to protecting and improving the wellbeing of workers believing that a healthy and safe work environment is also good for business.
