The Institute of Administrative Management (IAM), established in 1915, is one of the UK’s oldest management institutes. It focuses on developing professional administrators, managers, and business leaders. Through membership services and professional development programs, IAM helps individuals enhance their skills, build successful careers, and reach their full potential.

Whether you’re starting out or have years of experience, IAM offers tailored membership benefits. In today’s job market, effective administration skills are essential across both private and public sectors.

IAM has partnered with Croner-i to provide comprehensive support, ensuring professionals have the necessary skills and compliance guidance for success in their roles.
